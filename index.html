About this site

This site was built to replace the capability of Instapaper's read later functionality after they got rid of the auto-archive feature. It uses HTML5 client-side databases so no information is stored on our server, it's stored in your browser. Links are deleted after 30 days.

It's called FIFO Links because you get a First-In First-Out queue of your links to read later.

This site was made by @thadd.
